About Us

Who are we?

At BANT Designs, we believe that every washroom should be a work of art, reflecting the unique style and personality of the space and its occupants. That's why we approach every project with creativity, attention to detail, and a passion for innovative design.
Our design philosophy is built on a foundation of quality, sustainability, and functionality. We use only the highest-quality materials and finishes, and we are committed to eco-friendly practices and products.
